Deep Fried Oreos

Description

A deep-fried Oreo is a dessert or snack consisting of a chocolate sandwich cookie which is dipped in batter and deep fried. It may be served with different toppings, most commonly powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil (for frying) + 2 tsp (for batter)
  • Oreos
  • 1.5 cups Pancake Mix
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in fryer to 375Â°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk oil egg, and milk together.
  3. Stir in pancake mix and mix until there are no more clumps.
  4. Dip Oreos in batter with a fork and then place in hot oil. Fry until golden brown (it took about 2 minutes).
  5. Place Oreos on a paper towel-lined plate
  6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and ENJOY!
